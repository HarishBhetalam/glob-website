Child Safety Policy

Our Commitment

Glob has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content. We are committed to maintaining a safe platform for all users.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on Glob:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Content that exploits or endangers children
  • Grooming behavior targeting minors
  • Any content that violates child safety laws

Enforcement

Glob takes the following actions against CSAE violations:

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies

Reporting

To report CSAE content on Glob:

  • Tap the ⋯ menu on any video and select Report
  • Email us at harish.bhetalam50@gmail.com
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours

Age Requirements

Glob requires all users to be 16 or older. We do not knowingly allow minors under 16 to create accounts.
