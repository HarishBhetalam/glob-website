Delete Your Glob Account

How to delete

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Glob app
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap “Delete Account”
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your profile, name and username
  • All videos you have uploaded
  • Your likes, comments and followers
  • Your push notification token
  • Your account credentials

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal and security purposes before being permanently deleted.
