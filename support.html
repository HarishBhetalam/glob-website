Support

Need a hand? Browse the most common questions below, or reach out to us directly.

How do I sign up?

Tap "Sign up with email" on the welcome screen and follow the steps.

How do I switch countries?

Slide the country wheel at the bottom of the screen to browse different countries.

How do I browse by category?

Tap the white country pill to enter the feed, then tap it again to see category options like Travel, Food, Dance and more.

How do I upload a video?

Go to your profile and tap the + button to upload a video from your gallery.

How do I report a video?

Tap the ⋯ button on any video and select Report.

How do I contact support?

Email us at contactwebesen@gmail.com
